Small Business Seminars by Joe Moughon-CPA

 

Income Tax for Small Business Owners Seminar by Joe Moughon CPA

This course is centered on one statement:

Thus my primary purpose is to save you money, period!

Owning and operating your own business is the last of the great tax shelters and you will learn hundreds of tax deductions and tax saving strategies.

  • Learn the number one tax strategy that alone can save you thousands of dollars each year.

  • Learn legal, moral and ethical ways to deduct your fun, travel, meals and how to turn your automobile and home into tax-deductible gold mines.

  • This course covers how to audit proof your tax return with proper record-keeping and documentation of income, deductions and expenses.

  • Gain valuable insights in potential ordinary, necessary and reasonable expenses such as home office deductions, travel and entertainment expenses, employing your children or spouse, retirement plans, automobile deductions, and general operating expenses.

  • The course will also cover the tax ramifications of your business entity choice, estimated tax payments and an overview of how to maintain your company's records using QuickBooks.

Joe Moughon, CPA, has helped hundreds of entrepreneurs achieve their American dream. He works with local businesses and individuals in the areas of marketing, accounting, taxation, planning and coaching.
