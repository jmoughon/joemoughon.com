As a QuickBooks Pro Advisor, I have taught QuickBooks training classes in Houston and Galveston County, Texas, as well as, trained many local small business owners and their staff in their offices.  I would welcome the opportunity to show you what QuickBooks could do for your business or review your current use of QuickBooks.

Please keep this in mind.  All small businesses must have some sort of accounting system. 

QuickBooks gives you “clarity” for the past, present and future with the click of a mouse.

As a small business owner you have got to know the following “SEVEN KEY NUMBERS” at all times:

  • Check book balance-that is not that same as the balance per your bank

  • Accounts receivable-who owes you money and how long have they owed you

  • Accounts payable-who you owe money and how long have you owed them

  • Break-even point-how much you need to generate in monthly sales just to break-even

  • Monthly overhead-this is your monthly fixed costs just to open the doors each month, this may be close to the break-even point, if you don’t have a cost of sales

  • Average monthly sales and are your sales seasonal in nature

  • When do you file and owe taxes-sales tax, payroll tax, income tax, and property tax

Use QuickBooks for:

  • Check Writing

  • Sales Invoicing

  • Vendor Bill Paying

  • Payroll Processing and Payroll Reporting

  • Inventory

  • Financial Reporting

  • Customer Lists and Mailings

  • Internal Controls

  • Knowing where you stand at all times
